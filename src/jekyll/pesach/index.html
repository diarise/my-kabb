On the 15th of Nissan (Aries), the Creator is revealed in totality and completeness embracing humanity with Spiritual Light. This is the holiday of Pesach. As it says in the Haggadah: “On that night I will pass through the land of Egypt. I, and not an angel; I, and not a seraph; I, and not a deputy. I am God. I, and no other.”

Using the technology that is the Seder in all of its fifteen steps, the matzah, the wine, and of course the story telling of the Haggadah, we experience the full revelation of Light, freedom from our selfish desires as our Egypt, our Desire to Receive for the Self Alone, is removed from us.
